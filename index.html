About FoodSafetyHub

An innovative MCA 1st year project developed at IIIT Bhopal to revolutionize food safety testing

Heuristic Analysis

Comprehensive evaluation of food products based on multiple safety parameters including ingredients, expiry, storage conditions, and FSSAI compliance.

ML Predictions

Advanced k-NN machine learning algorithm to predict food safety based on pH, moisture, and temperature parameters.

Statistical Testing

Z-test and T-test implementations for laboratory data analysis with configurable confidence levels.
